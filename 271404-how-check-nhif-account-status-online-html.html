How to check your NHIF status in 2024: A step-by-step guide

The National Hospital Insurance Fund is a government-sponsored medical insurance scheme. The program accepts anyone who has attained the age of 18 years and has a monthly income of more than Kshs. 1000. NHIF registration is compulsory for those in the formal sector. This article explains how to check NHIF status online.

The Kenyan government established NHIF (National Hospital Insurance Fund) in 1966. The scheme protects citizens from unexpected and high medical costs. Employers in the formal sector must register their workers with NHIF. Self-employed and those in the informal sector can choose to register or not to join the program.

How to check NHIF status

NHIF has penalties for late payments. Your membership is reactivated after you pay the arrears plus penalties. You will also pay an NHIF card reactivation fee. You should track your monthly NHIF contributions and check your balance, arrears, and penalties. You can check NHIF balance at the nearest Huduma Centres, NHIF branches, via email, SMS, on the NHF website, and through the NHIF App.

How can I check my NHIF status online?

NHIF service delivery became more efficient after they adopted an online system. Many no longer visit NHIF offices or Huduma Centre to check NHIF status. You can seamlessly check NHIF statements online from wherever you are. Learn how to make an NHIF account status online inquiry below:

1. How to check NHIF status online on the website?

NHIF informs the public about its services, registration process, payment plans, terms & conditions, and more on its official website. Registered members can perform an NHIF status check online free of charge on the website.

NHIF members can directly log into the NHIF selfcare portal and check their status. You do not re-register when checking your status. Please follow this guide to check NHIF account status:

  • The system will take you to the NHIF status login page.
  • Enter your national ID number.
  • Click "Get One Time Pin."
  • Use the Pin to log into your NHIF account within 24 hours.
  • Proceed to check your NHIF status.
  • You will see your monthly contributions, penalties, and arrears.

Someone who is not an NHIF member must register and pay their monthly contributions. They can check their account statuses later. So, how does one register an NHIF account? Here is the registration process:

  • Visit NHIF's official website.
  • Click on "How can I register as a self-employed member with NHIF?" or "How can I register as an employed member with NHIF?"
  • Tap "click here," and the system will redirect you to the registration page.
  • Click "How To" to read the registration requirements and guidelines.
  • Click "Employer" or "Employee" to complete the registration form.
  • Attach scanned copies of the required documents and click "Submit."
  • The system will notify you of the application status.
  • You will also receive an SMS notification.
  • After completing the online registration process, visit the nearest NHIF office or Huduma Center for finger biometric registration. Bring with you the required documents and Kshs. 1,500.

2. How to check NHIF balance online on the app?

You can check your NHIF account status online through the 'My NHIF' mobile app. The app is available on Google Play and App Store. Download and install it on your phone, then register or log in to check your NHIF status.

3. How to check the NHIF status on the phone?

You can check NHIF account contribution status through a phone's messaging app. The service is accurate and convenient if you enter the correct details. Here is how to check NHIF status via SMS:

  • Open your phone's messaging app.
  • Type ID > skip a space > ID number or Passport number (e.g., ID 123456).
  • Send the text to NHIF status check number 21101.
  • You will receive information about your NHIF account status via SMS.

You will pay Kshs. 10 from your airtime to check the NHIF account status. Please use the phone number to register for this medical insurance scheme.

1. The NHIF online selfcare platform and app

You can initiate payments from the NHIF self-service portal or app. The system allows you to choose a payment option (bank account or mobile money).

You will receive an acknowledgment report when NHIF gets your money. Here is how to initiate the payment process on the portal or app and pay from M-Pesa:

  • Login to the NHIF self-service portal or mobile app.
  • Click "Payment."
  • Select "Type of Payment."
  • Enter the amount to pay.
  • Enter the mobile number to pay from.
  • Wait for an M-Pesa PIN prompt.
  • Enter your M-Pesa PIN and click "Send."
  • You will receive M-Pesa and NHIF payment confirmation messages.

2. How to pay for NHIF from your bank account?

NHIF can directly deduct your monthly contribution from your bank account if you authorize a bank standing order. Banks also have apps that allow you to pay NHIF monthly contributions, penalties, and arrears.

3. How to pay for NHIF using M-Pesa?

Use NHIF PayBill Number 200222 to transfer money from your M-Pesa account to the National Hospital Insurance Fund. Here are the procedures to follow:

  • Put your Safaricom line into your phone.
  • Go to the "M-Pesa menu."
  • Select "Lipa na M-Pesa."
  • Select "Pay bill."
  • Enter NHIF M-Pesa pay bill number 200222.
  • Enter your national ID number as the account number.
  • Key in the amount (NHIF payment monthly contribution). The system can only accept a maximum of Kshs. 6,000.
  • Enter your M-Pesa PIN and press "Send."
  • You will receive M-Pesa and NHIF payment confirmation messages.

Please note if you use your national ID number as your Account Number and not your NHIF member number, you must pay premiums up to the current month for your cover to be active. Safaricom’s terms and conditions apply.

4. How to pay for NHIF using Safaricom Bonga points?

You can redeem Safaricom Bonga points to pay for NHIF. Dial *126#, choose "Lipa Na Bonga," then use PayBill Number 200222 and your ID number as the account number. Choose the number of points you wish to redeem and follow the prompts to complete the process.

5. How to pay for NHIF using Airtel Money?

Use NHIF PayBill Number 823263 to pay your National Hospital Insurance Fund contributions, arrears, and penalties. Here are the steps for paying the NHIF bill through Airtel Money Kenya:

  • Enter your Airtel line into your phone.
  • Go to the "Airtel Money menu."
  • Select "Make payments."
  • Select "Pay Bills."
  • Select "Other."
  • Enter NHIF pay bill number 823263.
  • Type in the amount you wish to pay.
  • Enter your Airtel Money PIN.
  • Use your ID number as a reference.
  • Click send and wait for Airtel and NHIF payment confirmation messages.

6. How to pay NHIF through Equitel Money?

Equity Bank account holders can use their Equitel Money lines to pay their NHIF contributions, arrears, and penalties. Here is how you can use an Equitel phone number to pay for NHIF:

  • Enter your Equitel line into your phone.
  • Go to the "Equitel menu."
  • Select “My Money.”
  • Choose “Send/Pay.”

How much do self-employed people contribute to NHIF?

NHIF monthly contribution rates for employees in the formal sector are based on 2% of an individual's gross income. The maximum cap is Kshs. 1,700, and it is for those earning Kshs. 100,000 or above.

The self-employed NHIF members pay Kshs. 500 monthly. The new HIF draft rules will require the self-employed to pay a special contribution of 2.75% of the declared or assessed gross monthly income, subject to a minimum of Kshs. 300.

How does one update an NHIF profile?

You can make numerous NHIF status updates, including adding and removing dependants (children and spouses) or updating their records.

How to change a spouse on your NHIF profile?

You will be required to produce these documents or more to the nearest NHIF office or Huduma Centre, depending on the case:

  • Copy of ID for both parties
  • Divorce certificate
  • Death certificate for a deceased spouse
  • Colored passport-size photo (s)

How to check NHIF dependants online?

You can update or amend NHIF depandents online instead of visiting the nearest NHIF office or Huduma Centre. You need these documents:

  • A copy of your national ID.
  • A copy of your spouse (s) national ID.
  • A photocopy of your marriage certificate or a sworn affidavit from a magistrate if it is a traditional union.
  • You should register for an additional NHIF card to cover your other wife if she was not included in the initial NHIF card.
  • A copy of your child’s birth certificate.
  • Use a birth notification if the kid is below six months.
  • Colored passport-size photos for your dependants.

How to add dependants to your NHIF card online?

  • Log in to your account via the NHIF website or app.
  • Click "Dependant’s registration."
  • Enter your national ID number.
  • Fill in all the other sections.
  • Attach a scanned copy of the dependant’s photo (colored and passport-size).
  • Attach a scanned copy of the dependant's birth certificate/ marriage certificate/affidavit.
  • Click "Save" to save the documents
  • Click "Submit."
  • Note that you can only submit the request if all fields are filled out.
  • The system sends a notification to the contributor’s phone number to keep a tab on the submission status.

How to add dependants to your NHIF card at the NHIF office or Huduma Center?

  • Download an NHIF 26 Amendment Form online or get it from an NHIF office or Huduma Center.
  • Fill out all the relevant sections and sign the form. Remember to state the needed amendments-adding dependants to NHIF.
  • Attach all required documents to the NHIF-dependant registration form.
  • Hand the forms to the relevant officer at an NHIF branch or Huduma Centre.
  • The officer will verify the documents and enter some information into the system.
  • The officer will fill out the "For official use only" section on the NHIF 26 – Amendment Form.
  • Afterward, NHIF will update the new information about your dependants in your account.

How do I know my balance in NHIF?

You can type "ID > leave a space > ID or Passport number" and text the message to NHIF number 21101. Alternatively, dial USSD code *155# and follow the prompts, request an NHIF statement via customercare@nhif.or.ke. You can also check your balance by logging into your account via the NHIF website or mobile app.

Is NHIF status check online free?

Checking your NHIF status via the NHIF app, website, USSD code *155#, and email is free.

How much does it cost to check NHIF status?

You only pay Ksh. 10 from your airtime (Safaricom, Airtel, or Telkom) if you send an NHIF status check message to 21101 to inquire about your account's status.
